### What is a knowledge base?

A knowledge base is a collection of information that is organized in a structured way. It can include FAQs, documentation, web pages, and more. Knowledge bases are essential for providing accurate and relevant information to your customers.



### How to train your AI on your knowledge base?
You can ingest your knowledge base into Open with the following steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to Open">
    You can log in to Open using your email and password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Knowledge Base page">
    It's available on the left side of the Open dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new knowledge base">
    You will see a popup with all the available integrations. Select the one you want to use and follow the instructions to add your knowledge base.
  </Step>
</Steps>
