## Confluence Integration

Connect your Confluence workspace to power your AI with your team's knowledge:
- Access all Confluence pages and spaces
- Real-time content syncing
- Smart search across documentation
- Automatic updates when pages change

### Setup Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Confluence">
    Link your Atlassian account from the Open.cx dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Spaces">
    Choose which Confluence spaces to include:
    - Documentation spaces
    - Team spaces
    - Project spaces
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Sync">
    Set up your sync preferences:
    - Sync frequency
    - Content filters
    - Access permissions
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Features
- Automatic content synchronization
- Deep search capabilities
- Version history tracking
- Permission-aware access
