## Google Drive Integration

Turn your Google Drive into an AI knowledge source:
- Access documents and spreadsheets
- Process shared drives
- Real-time document updates
- Smart content extraction

### Setup Process

<Steps>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Content">

    - Documents
    - Spreadsheets
    - Presentations
    - Shared drives
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Access">
    Set up access permissions and sync settings
  </Step>
</Steps>
